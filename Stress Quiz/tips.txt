Tips for sleeping better
//http://www.cdc.gov/sleep/about_sleep/sleep_hygiene.html

1. Keep a consistent sleep schedule. Try to go to bed and wake up around the same time every day.
2. Make your bedroom dark, quiet, and comfortable.



For exercising too little:
//http://www.mayoclinic.org/healthy-lifestyle/stress-management/in-depth/exercise-and-stress/art-20044469

Exercise boosts endorphins, the neurotransmitters that make you feel good. Exercising can help relieve stress! To get started: 
1. Ease into a routine--don't push yourself too hard, too fast. Build your strength!
2. Do something fun! Whether you prefer jogging, tennis, rock climbing, or yoga, make exercise something you enjoy
3. Make an exercise schedule
4. Set goals
5. Exercise with friends! 
6. Remember, short increments are just as good as longer workout sessions.
